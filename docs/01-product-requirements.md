# JourneyAI Product Requirements Document

## Overview

JourneyAI is a travel intelligence platform focused on improving the journey experience, not just reaching the destination.

Traditional navigation systems mainly optimize routes based on time and distance.

JourneyAI focuses on:

- Route discovery
- Travel experience
- Places along routes
- Personalized journeys


---

# Problem Statement

Users often want routes based on preferences beyond shortest distance:

Examples:

- Scenic routes
- Better roads
- Food stops
- Cafes
- Hotels
- Fuel stations
- Places to explore


Existing map applications provide navigation but limited journey personalization.

---

# Goal

Build an intelligent journey planning platform that helps users choose routes based on:

- Time
- Distance
- Traffic
- Road quality
- Nearby facilities
- Travel experience
